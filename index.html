Posta da Kona is a bookshop and a shared studio in Tbilisi, that unites Posta Press and Kona Books under one roof. Bookshop has a selection of our own produce, that focuses mainly on Georgian Culture, Art and Architecture and as well publications of other independent publishers from around the world.
