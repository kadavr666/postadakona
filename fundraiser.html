Posta da Kona Fundraiser
Posta da Kona bookshop is releasing editioned artworks and drawings sales in support of our fundraising efforts. These editions and drawings are an important source of revenue for our organization, proceeds from the sale directly underwrite the production and printing costs of ongoing projects.This limited-time sale features works by Ketuta-Alexi Meskhishvili, Thea Gvetadze, Thea Djordjadze, Stefanie Heinze, Nina Kintsurashvili, Tolia Astakhishvili, David Meskhi, Mariam Kalabegashvili, Tony Just, Sasha Zalivako and Gvantsa Jgushia.

If you would like more information about the works or require an estimate for shipping fees, please call us at info@konabooks.ge
We accept payment by Paypal, Wise, bank transfer or credit card.
